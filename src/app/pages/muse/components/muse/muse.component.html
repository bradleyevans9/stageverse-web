What is Muse: Enter The Simulation?

This is a mixed reality experience of the Simulation Theory world tour, captured at the sold out Madrid’s Wanda Metropolitano Stadium. You can meet up with friends and fellow fans at the Muse virtual venue in the Stageverse as avatars, and experience the show from over 17 different viewpoints around the stadium.
